Our Services

We Provide Best Industrial Services

Danmisho Nigeria

Providing hydraulic repair and rebuild services for all brands of industrial hydraulic equipment.

DANMISHO Nigeria offers comprehensive Engine Overhauling and Diagnostic Solutions, designed to keep your heavy equipment and machinery operating at peak performance. With our years of experience and a team of highly skilled technicians, we provide a full range of services that ensure the longevity and reliability of your engines, even in the most demanding environments.

Our engine overhauling service involves a thorough inspection and complete disassembly of your engine, followed by a meticulous rebuild to restore it to optimal working condition. Whether you're dealing with wear and tear, engine breakdowns, or performance issues, our team utilizes advanced tools and techniques to diagnose and address underlying problems. We replace or refurbish key components, such as pistons, crankshafts, cylinder heads, and turbochargers, to ensure your engine runs smoothly and efficiently.

At DANMISHO, we understand that engine failure or reduced performance can lead to costly downtime. That’s why we provide diagnostic solutions that help identify potential issues before they become major problems. Our diagnostic services include advanced fault detection using cutting-edge technology and diagnostic tools, ensuring that we pinpoint the root cause of any engine-related issues. From electrical system malfunctions to mechanical failures, we offer precise and accurate diagnostics that help prevent unexpected breakdowns and minimize operational disruptions.

Our experienced technicians use both traditional methods and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to assess engine health and performance. By conducting thorough engine diagnostics, we can identify critical issues such as compression loss, fuel system malfunctions, coolant leaks, and exhaust problems. This proactive approach allows us to provide targeted solutions that restore engine efficiency, reduce fuel consumption, and enhance overall performance.

We also offer preventive maintenance services, which are designed to keep your engines running smoothly between overhauls. Our maintenance programs include regular inspections, oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid checks, all aimed at preventing wear and tear and extending the life of your engines. Our commitment to proactive care ensures that your equipment continues to perform at its best, minimizing the risk of costly repairs and unexpected breakdowns.

DANMISHO Nigeria's engine overhauling and diagnostic solutions are not limited to a specific type or brand of engine. We provide services for a wide range of engines, including those used in construction, mining, agriculture, and industrial machinery. Whether you have a small generator engine or a large industrial diesel engine, our team is equipped to handle your needs with precision and care.

Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction ensures that every engine overhaul and diagnostic service is performed to the highest standards. We use only the best replacement parts, ensuring that your engine operates with the same efficiency and reliability as it did when it was new. Our goal is to minimize downtime, optimize engine performance, and help you avoid costly repairs down the line.

Trust DANMISHO Nigeria for all your engine overhauling and diagnostic needs. Our expertise, commitment to excellence, and customer-first approach make us the ideal partner to ensure your machinery operates at its best, every time.
