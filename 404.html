About Us

We Are Leader In Industrial Market

DANMISHO NIGERIA is an indigenous mechanical engineering company with over 15 years in maintenance of all kinds of heavy industrial machineries and diesel engine services for container terminals, construction industries, and the oil and gas.

Read more

The company was formally registered and incorporated with the Corporate Affairs Commission in 2014. The company operation base is at Port Harcourt, Nigeria, but now, also offer services in Ghana, Accra.

We are specialist in offering reliable general mechanical and hydraulic maintenance as well as electrical services on all heavy-duty equipment and machineries like Reachstackers, Cranes, trucks, tractors, excavators, graders Forklift as well as power plants like Catgen, Perkins, Cumins, turbines and air compressors etc.

Our Objectives

  • The mission of DANMISHO NIGERIA ENTERPRISES is to provide high quality, convenient and comprehensive mechanical repair at low cost but yet reliable services to our clients.
  • Targeted 100% customer satisfaction regarding quality, friendliness, prompt service delivery and to explore innovative ways to exceed the expectation of our client.
  • To meet our client need with the best global standards
  • To carry out operation with zero negative effects on the environment and adopting ALARP where zero impact cannot be achieved.

Our Vision

What We Aspire To Achieve

  • To become a major indigenous mechanical engineering and hydraulic services provider to the maritime, constructions, manufacturing, the oil and gas industries across West Africa, delivering top quality and technically sound services, cost saving while ensuring a safe operating environment.
  • Managing a pool of resources and technically balanced and experienced personnel that can meet the needs of our client as well as growing challenges.
